About me

I am a fifth year PhD student in Statistics at the University of Washington, with primary interest in the field of high dimensional statistics. My advisor is Mathias Drton, with whom I have been working on various problems related to latent factor models. Some of these problems fall into the area of algebraic statistics, a maturing field that seeks to study statistical models with tools in algebraic geometry.
