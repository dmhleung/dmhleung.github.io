About me

I am currently a postdoctoral scholar working in the Department of Data Sciences and Operations at the University of Southern California, under the supervision of Professor Wenguang Sun and Professor Tony Cai from University of Pennsylvania. I completed my PhD in statistics at University of Washington in 2016, advised by Professor Mathias Drton. I have a general interest in high-dimensional data analysis, as well as the emerging field of algebraic statistics.
